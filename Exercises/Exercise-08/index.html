A mortal named Han Li embarks on a long, treacherous journey to become an immortal cultivator. Relying on wit, caution, and perseverance, he defies fate in a world where power reigns supreme.

Set in the chaotic final years of the Tang Dynasty, this story follows the mysterious Unorthodox Sect as they fight for survival and justice. Secrets, betrayal, and martial arts power struggles unfold in a vividly animated world.

Qin Mu, a boy raised by gods, ventures into the unknown to discover the truth of his past and reshape the future. Blending mythology, cultivation, and rebellion, this story unveils the rise of an unlikely hero.

Wang Lin, once an ordinary boy, turns to cultivation after tragedy strikes his family. With deep determination and a fierce heart, he walks a lonely, ruthless path toward ultimate power and revenge.
